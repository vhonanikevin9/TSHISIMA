Our Mission

Our mission is to promote a love for literature and preserve indigenous languages, inspiring a new generation of readers and writers. We aim to create a platform where local authors can share their stories, and readers can connect with their cultural heritage.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a society where indigenous languages and cultures thrive, and communities come together to celebrate their heritage through literature. We aim to inspire a love for reading and writing in local languages, preserving cultural identity and promoting social cohesion.

What We Do

We are active in promoting indigenous literature and language preservation.

Book Club

Discussing books written in indigenous languages to promote cultural heritage.

Manuscript Pub

Free publication for disabled authors in Tshivenda, Xitsonga, Sepedi & English.

Literacy Activities

Workshops and events to promote literacy and a love for reading.

Book Purchases

Buying books from local authors to grow our in-house library.

Book Reviews

Participating in book review competitions by local Authors.

Community Events

Attending events like The Book Club Affair to promote reading.
